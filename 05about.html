History

Almost 30 Years of Buisness!


2012: The Bridal Suite of Hamilton is owned and operated by Kimberly Ostrowski.

2007,2008,2009: The Bridal Suite rated "Exceptional" in The Knot magazine, ranking higher than Klienfeld's in NYC! Check out The Knot's: Best of Weddings Issue.

Winter 2007-08: Renovation of additional 2200 square foot building of boutique space dedicated to bridal party and specialty gowns.

2003: Owners Kimberly Ostrowski and Danielle Nutt are long time childhood friends. Danielle, who has worked as a wedding and special event coordinator, joins Kim a full-time guidance counselor, to rejuvenate an already successful bridal business.

1999-2002: The Bridal Suite is owned by Cindy Jones.

1985-1999: The Bridal Suite is owned by Marie DeAngelo.
